Selection Criteria

We take a disciplined approach to investing

Management

We love working with founders who have a strong thesis on what, why, and how their companies are trying to execute, yet who are receptive to our talent-focused playbook. We value clear vision, adaptability, thirst for knowledge, and ability to attract and retain high-performance talent

Model

We prioritize business models that can rapidly scale in a capital efficient manner and win with data over code. We respect businesses with long-term customers and high margins. Our consumer investments favor customer acquisition through performance marketing and smart inventory models

Market

We believe in businesses bringing industry leading solutions to large addressable markets. In general, we prefer markets that are not “winner takes all.” Categories in which we have recently invested include interior design, men’s grooming, women’s health, everyday apparel, and functional beverages

Momentum

We are excited by businesses with demonstrated revenue and high sequential growth. For companies yet to generate revenue, we look for other indicators of positive momentum, such as usage and customer satisfaction

Playbook

We have developed a proven-value playbook

Onboarding elite talent

Our team of executive search specialists provides founders with access to high-performing talent from top management consulting, investment banking and private equity firms

Building a data-driven culture

Our team of expert practitioners uses data science and process expertise to help founders build a data- driven culture

Leveraging intuitive design

Our team of designers, developers and marketing experts provides founders with branding, marketing, and website development services

Financial reporting capabilities

Our team of business analysts and chartered accountants provides founders with bookkeeping, reporting, business intelligence and financial modelling services
