Portfolio Advertising & Analytics Services

Guild Capital, through its operating affiliate Support Platform LLC, provides advertising analytics and reporting services to the companies in our portfolio. Support Platform LLC operates the portfolio analytics platform on behalf of Guild Capital and its portfolio companies.

Our Services

What we provide to our portfolio

Advertising performance reporting

Cross-platform reporting across Amazon Ads, Meta, TikTok, and Google Ads, surfaced in unified views for each portfolio company.

KPI and analytics dashboards

Dashboards consolidating advertising spend, sales, and customer metrics so portfolio teams can see performance and growth in one place.

Ad spend analytics

Spend analytics and budget optimization recommendations grounded in each portfolio company's own advertising data.

Campaign performance reviews

Regular campaign performance reviews and growth reporting delivered to portfolio company management teams.

Access

For Guild Capital portfolio companies

These services are provided to Guild Capital portfolio companies, each of which operates its own advertising accounts. If you are a portfolio company and would like access to the analytics platform, contact data@guildcap.com.
